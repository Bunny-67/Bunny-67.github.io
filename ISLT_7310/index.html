WELCOME
I AM IAN RAY
A DIGITAL MEDIA SPECIALIST
AND THIS IS MY PORTFOLIO

For the last five years, as a freelance digital media specialist,
I have been working hard to provide my clients quality work for their
individual endeavors. This portfolio site contains the work I have
created for them as well as some of the personal work that I have done.
As a media specialist I aim to have the ability to create any form of
digital media that one could need to pursue their desired online presence.
I have worked in photography, videography, graphic design, animation,
website design, social media marketing, and more. With proficiencies in
the Adobe Creative Suite, I strive to be prepared for my clients' digital
needs without having to outsource any work. I like to take a personal and
attentive approach to my creations, and truly deliver content
that benefits the client.
