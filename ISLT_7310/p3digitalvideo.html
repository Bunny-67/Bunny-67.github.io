This presentation serves as my 2023 demo reel, curated to highlight a diverse array of projects completed over the recent years. The primary objective of this demo reel is to underscore my proficiency of various applications within the Adobe Creative Cloud Suite, such as Adobe Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. Additionally, while not explicitly showcased in the video, I possess proficiency in additional Adobe applications such as Audition, Light Room, and InDesign.

The content shown in this demo reel represents my skills and expertise, providing a glimpse into the integration of creativity and technical proficiency that I strive to define in my work. All of the videos featured in this demo reel can be found (soon) on the Videography project page.

ISLT_4631

Introduction and Learning Objectives
The objective of this project was to revitalize and refresh the demo reel on my website and expand upon the abilities showcased in a previous reel. I used this project to accumulate my works in to a single demonstration and attempted to display numerous editing techniques. The alternative purpose of this project was to create a platform video that is easy to expand and improve. The editing done for this video specifically utilizes music that can be altered and repeated in order to fill the necessary time period, and utilizes clip segments that can be reordered and altered if desired.

Licensing
The only unoriginal media in this project is a track named "Ringside" created by Dyalla. This track is a free, commercial use track provided by the public YouTube Audio Library that requires no attribution in online usage. All other content is curated from personal projects.

Techniques
•Changed Track Decibel Volumes
•Inserted Background Ambient Audio
•Inserted Exponential Fade Out to Background Audio
•Utilized Custom Made Negative Space Graphics Created in Photoshop
•Trimmed and Spaced Audio Layers
•Applied Numerous Crossfades and Transitions
•Inserted Active Text
•Inserted Motion on Different Image Elements
•Applied Numerous Crossfades and Transitions

Reflection
This project was a great opportunity to improve and recreate my demo reel from scratch. I created this video in order to replace my old and outdated demo reel as well as proudly introduce a video that I can showcase on my website, as well as use as a base to improve upon in the future. This reel features my work from multiple Adobe Creative Cloud programs and demonstrates a small portion of my proficiency with each.
