In this project, I had the opportunity to create media for Harmony Chiropractic, a chiropractic firm in South Elgin, IL. For this I took on all of the roles necessary to produce a finished video product, including directing, filming, lighting, sound design, video editing and graphic creation. I filmed and edited 8 total videos spanning anywhere between 30sec to 3min. Harmony has used these videos to market on their Facebook and Instagram pages. Additionally, it was a great chance to branch into a new category of both filming and editing styles. The project lasted 6 months in total and utilized Adobe Premiere, Photoshop, and Audition.
